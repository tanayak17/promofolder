The Haunting Begins

In a forgotten Victorian-era Indian mansion shrouded in eerie fog, three steampunk-inspired ghost hunters arrive, armed with intricate ghost detectors and electric lanterns.

The once-glorious palace, now a haunted ruin under the full moon, conceals horrors beyond their comprehension.

"Some spirits refuse to be forgotten"

Faces in the Dark

The Hunters

Armed with intricate ghost detectors and electric lanterns, they venture where others dare not go.

The Woman in Red

A mysterious figure in a red sari standing at the end of a dimly lit corridor, her face obscured.

The Mirror

An old, dust-covered mirror etched with blood-red inscriptions revealing a sinister secret.

Fractured Memories

As past and present collide, they realize that some hauntings are not just echoes of the past but fractured memories clawing their way into reality.

Some spirits refuse to be forgotten, and some memories should remain buried.
